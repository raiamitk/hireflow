How signals are computed from 468 landmarks
Eye contact — iris offset from eye centre, normalised by eye width. Both eyes averaged over 15 frames.
Brow furrow — inner brow (lm 22/52) distance from nose bridge (lm 168), normalised by face height.
Smile — lip corner distance (lm 61/291) normalised by face width. Rolling 20-frame average.
Confidence — 0.4×eye + 0.3×(1−stress) + 0.3×smile.
Stress — 0.5×brow + 0.3×(1−eye) + 0.2×(1−engagement).
Engagement — head pose yaw/pitch deviation from neutral (nose tip vs nose base).
While running, all signals are written to window.HireFlowFaceSignals — plug this into hireflow-v2.html by enabling Face Mesh in the interview screen.
